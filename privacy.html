Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to 1Save! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app and services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: If you sign up for a premium account, we may collect your email address and payment details.
  • Saved Content: URLs, folder names, and metadata you save within the app.
  • Device Information: Basic device data (e.g., operating system, app version) to ensure compatibility and improve performance.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to provide and improve our services:

  • Sync your saved content across devices (for premium users).
  • Backup your data securely to prevent loss.
  • Analyze app performance and fix issues.

Data Storage and Security

We use a Local-First approach, meaning your data is primarily stored on your device. For all users, saved links and associated metadata are collected and securely stored. For premium users, data is synced to Google Cloud Firestore for backup and multi-device access. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure authentication protocols.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and export your data.
  • Delete your account and associated data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@1save.app.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services like Firebase for authentication and Firestore for data backup. These services comply with strict data protection regulations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@1save.app.
