Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how 1Save ("we", "us", or "the App") handles your information. 1Save is built with a "Privacy by Design" philosophy.

1. Data Collection & Usage

Local Content: 1Save is a local-first utility. All links you save, folder names you create, and passwords you set are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to our servers.

No Account Required: You do not need to create an account to use 1Save. Therefore, we do not collect your name, email address, or phone number.

2. Security & Encryption

The "Secret Folder" feature uses local encryption. Because we do not store your passwords on a server, we cannot recover your password if you lose it. Your security is entirely under your control.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with third parties. The App does not contain third-party advertising or analytics SDKs that track your personal behavior.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored on your device, you can delete all your data at any time by simply uninstalling the App or clearing the App's cache in your Android settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: support@1save.app
